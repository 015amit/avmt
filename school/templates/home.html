Vision

Founded in 1997, Adarsh Vidya Mandir has grown to become a poineer in the education in Koderma, Jharkhand, located in Tilokari, Koderma, Jharkhnad. This school is for wholesome development of a child. Here, from 1st to 10th class is running since last 20 years.Every year, our 10th board result has been outstanding. We not only provide education, we inculcate ethics, manner and social values in each student so that he/she can contributes in developement of country and society. We have science lab, sport facilities, liberary. We provide free education for poor students too.

Mission

Our goal is to focus on the holistic development of each child, and to give them a competitive edge with the help of an extensive curriculum and dynamic teaching methodologies.
